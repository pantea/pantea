Private Classes

Are you looking to progress faster?

Then you should consider taking Private Lessons. In a private lesson you work directly on your needs. This individual form of instruction is the most efficient way to improve your technique, your connection, your rhythm, and your styling.

You may share your lessons with a partner (one leader, one follower) at no additional fee! Discounted private packages are available in groups of 5, 10, 15, 20, 30 and 50.

Packages are also available for Wedding dance choreography.

Use this form for inquiries or to schedule a private class.
